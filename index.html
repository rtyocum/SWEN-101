Volunteer Now

Our goal is to bring together the local community and make volunteer work simpler, and more engaging. Our prototype, Volunteer Now, aims to gain a new perspective on volunteer work by gamifying the industry. We plan to market our product to college students who may be new to volunteering or want to compete with others. We were inspired by the point-based systems of successful educational websites such as Duolingo, and Khan Academy, which incentivise users to maintain their progress.
